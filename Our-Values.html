Our Values

Rooted in Revelation 3:7,These values shape our identity and mission as a church family:

Holiness

We live consecrated lives, set apart for God in thought, word, and action.

Truth

We uphold the Word of God as the final authority, living and teaching in His truth.

Faith in God’s Sovereignty

We trust God’s authority to open and close doors according to His perfect will.

Obedience

We walk faithfully through every door the Lord opens, responding with obedience to His call.

Perseverance

We endure challenges and remain steadfast in faith, even with little strength.

Love & Fellowship

We are a family bound by brotherly love, welcoming all with the compassion of Christ.
