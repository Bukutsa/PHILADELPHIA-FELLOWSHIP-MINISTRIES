About Us

We are a people set apart—not by perfection, but by a deep, ongoing surrender to Jesus. Marked by repentance, we turn daily from darkness to light, allowing His grace to shape us into vessels of holiness. Fueled by His love, we extend our arms to the broken, the searching, and the forgotten. In a world desperate for truth, we rise not to condemn it, but to illuminate it—with lives that echo heaven’s call and hearts that carry the compassion of Christ. This is not just a gathering. It’s a movement of grace, a pursuit of glory, and a bold step into the mission of God.
