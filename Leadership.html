Pastor Margaret Nyambane

“I am looking forward to the time when prophetic praise and worship shall be lifted up to our God to bring down His glory which will be manifested by massive healing and deliverance.”

Pastor Margaret Nyambane is the founder and lead pastor of Philadelphia Fellowship International Ministries. She is a dedicated servant of God with a passion for preaching the gospel of JESUS CHRIST, reaching out to the needy, the lowly, and the lost by bringing hope to the hopeless and perishing to build the great image of Christ Jesus in the living word of God. She is a gospel minister whose greatest prayer and desire is to minister first to God and then to the people of God as they seek to worship the Almighty Father in spirit and in truth.
